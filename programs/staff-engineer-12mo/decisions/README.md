## Template

```markdown
# Title

**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Status:** Accepted | Superseded | Deprecated

## Context
What problem were we solving and what were the constraints?

## Decision
What did we decide?

## Consequences
What becomes easier or harder as a result?
```
